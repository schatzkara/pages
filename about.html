Biography

Kara Schatz is a PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at North Carolina State University. Before starting her PhD, she completed her Bachelor’s degree in Mathematics and Computer Science at Xavier University in 2020. Her current research field is data and knowledge management and discovery. In particular, she is focused on developing approaches for discovering new knowledge through pattern mining in large datasets. Currently, she collaborates with biomedical experts at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill and is applying her work to address drug discovery. After completing her PhD, she hopes to find a job as a Computer Science Professor.
